We Create A Smile!

CDC (Community Dental Care) is a charitable non-profit self-funded organization based in MIU since 2015, consisting of more than 150 students.

Our aim is to spread dental awareness all over Egypt whilst treating as many patients as we can in our convoys through donations and fundraising campaigns.

VOLUNTEER

Our Activities

Events

Which are self-funded co-ordinated events in elderly homes and orphanages, Blankets and meals distributions all over Egypt & special events for Eid, Orphan's day,and hospital visits.

Online campaigns

CDC has many online campaigns aiming to educate people on their dental and oral health through various social media platforms, such as Facebook, Instagram, TikTok & Youtube.

Convoys

CDC dental convoys aim to reach poor villages and distant areas to provide them with needed dental care services in: pediatrics, operative, surgery. Recently we managed to add endodontic treatment as well.

Comic book

CDC's digital comic book features our unique characters (supermolar, gena7ato, etc.) that aim to spread dental awareness to children through interactive stories and games.

Student Discounts

Special deals exclusive to MIU students redeemed through their MIU IDs. Premium discounts are also available through CDC's premium card.
