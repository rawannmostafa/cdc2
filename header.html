We Create A Smile!

CDC (Community Dental Care) is a charitable non-profit self-funded organization based in MIU since 2015, consisting of more than 150 students.

Our aim is to spread dental awareness all over Egypt whilst treating as many patients as we can in our convoys through donations and fundraising campaigns.
